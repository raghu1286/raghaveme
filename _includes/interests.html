MyCognition

MyCognition is a cognitive health company which makes assessments and video games, that measure and train cognitive health. This project is centered around making use of the existing API but with a new UI. The new system has the ability to play games, view users within subscriptions and to report on a range of criteria based on cognitive health scores of users.

One of the challenging piece of work in this project was the generation of PDF reports of the analytics dashboard. It had to be the exact report as shown in the web-app, to achecive this I went with a headless browser phantomjs implmentation exposed through a nodejs web-service api.

  • Client: MyCognition
  • Date: March 2016
  • Domain: Health Science & Analytics
  • Technology: HTML5, BackboneJS, JSPM, Jquery, Bootstrap, Highcharts, Nodejs, ExpressJs PhantomJs, Java, Spring, MySql
